## Orders & Shipping

- Orders are processed within 1 business day.
- Standard shipping within India takes 3 to 5 business days.
- International shipping takes 10 to 14 business days.
- You can track your order through the Lumera Home app or via the tracking
  link emailed at the time of dispatch.

## Returns & Refunds

- Lumera Tech offers a **30-day return window** from the date of delivery.
- The product must be in its original packaging and unused condition.
- Refunds are processed within **5 to 7 business days** after we receive the
  returned item.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
- Return shipping is free for defective products. For change-of-mind returns,
  the customer covers return shipping.

## Warranty

- All Lumera Tech products come with a standard 2-year warranty covering
  parts and labor for manufacturing defects.
- The warranty does not cover damage from misuse, accidents, or unauthorized
  modifications.

## Account & Privacy

- You can manage your account, addresses, and payment methods through the
  Lumera Home app.
- Lumera Tech does not sell customer data to third parties.
